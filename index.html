Everything you need to know before joining.

How do the signals work? ▶
34 automated workflows scan 27+ instruments every 15 minutes using ICT/Smart Money Concepts. When a valid setup appears (scored 70+ out of 100), it posts to your Discord with M15 and H4 charts, entry, stop loss, take profit, and AI reasoning behind the trade. Every signal is also captured by our Signal Intelligence system for pattern tracking.
What is ICT/SMC? ▶
Inner Circle Trader / Smart Money Concepts. An institutional-grade methodology based on how banks and hedge funds actually move markets. Core concepts: Order Blocks, Fair Value Gaps, Liquidity Sweeps, Power of 3 (Accumulation → Manipulation → Distribution).
Do I need to watch charts all day? ▶
No. The system watches for you and sends alerts directly to Discord. You get mobile notifications on your phone. When you see a signal, you decide whether to take the trade. We provide the intelligence; you make the call.
What instruments do you cover? ▶
27+ instruments across 4 asset classes: Forex — GBP basket (GBPUSD, GBPJPY, GBPAUD, GBPCAD, GBPNZD, GBPCHF + EURGBP compass) plus majors (EURUSD, USDJPY, DXY, AUDUSD, USDCAD). Crypto — BTC, ETH, XRP, SOL, BNB, DOGE, ADA, LINK, LTC, DOT (10 coins, 24/7). Indices — Nasdaq, Dow, S&P 500. Commodities — Gold, Crude Oil. Every instrument is tracked by Signal Intelligence.

Yes. OXY AI has two kinds of memory. Personal memory: it remembers your conversations, the instruments you ask about, your trading level, and your preferences across sessions. Market memory: it tracks every signal it generates across all instruments, so when you ask about GBPUSD, it can tell you how many signals fired this week, the average grade, and the directional bias. Signal Intelligence is our self-learning market memory. Every signal OXY ALGO generates is captured, parsed, and stored in a structured database.
