Common Questions

Everything you need to know about the platform.

What exactly is OXY ALGO? ▶
OXY ALGO is an autonomous trading AI platform. It runs 42 automated workflows powered by 8 specialized AI agents that scan 38 instruments across 7 forex baskets, score setups using ICT/Smart Money Concepts, execute trades, manage risk, and track performance — all running 24/7 without human intervention. The platform is delivered through Discord with a GPT-4 powered AI assistant that supports natural conversation, screenshot analysis, and voice interaction.
How is this different from other trading tools? ▶
Most services are one person sending manual alerts from a Telegram group. OXY ALGO is fundamentally different: 8 AI agents working across 42 automated workflows scanning 38 instruments in 7 forex baskets, self-hosted infrastructure with 7 APIs, GPT-4 Vision for screenshot analysis, voice AI interaction, persistent memory that learns about each user, and Signal Intelligence that tracks every signal to verified outcome. Cross-pairs automatically post to both relevant baskets. We don't forget yesterday's calls — we have the receipts. Win rates, net pips, grade breakdowns, all verified by code.
What makes this infrastructure vs a signal service? ▶
A signal service gives you alerts. OXY ALGO gives you a complete autonomous system. 42 workflows running 24/7 that scan 38 instruments across 7 forex baskets, score, execute, track, and learn. Correlation Intelligence ranks 8 currencies and detects SMT Divergence in real-time. Cross-pairs automatically appear in both relevant baskets. A self-learning market memory verifies every signal to outcome. An AI assistant that speaks, sees screenshots, and remembers every conversation. Content Engine 2.0 generates ~36 emotionally-targeted videos per week. Trade Timelapse Engine captures chart evolution throughout every trade. This is infrastructure that runs whether you're watching or not.
What is the Content Bridge? ▶
Content Engine 2.0 is Layer 5 of the OXY ALGO architecture. It uses a 5-pillar emotional system (Pain, Proof, Education, Identity, Aspiration) to generate ~36 viral video packages per week from real trading data. Each pillar has unique hooks, SFX cues, and comment-driving CTAs. Three n8n workflows scan markets and generate emotionally-targeted scripts via Perplexity AI, with format randomizers, schedule jitter, and humanization rules to evade AI detection. The Trade Timelapse Engine captures chart screenshots every 15 minutes during live trades for video backgrounds. HeyGen creates avatar videos and Blotato distributes to 9 platforms.
What technology powers the platform? ▶
n8n for workflow orchestration (42 workflows). GPT-4 + GPT-4o Vision for analysis and image understanding. OpenAI Whisper for voice transcription. ElevenLabs for voice synthesis. TradingView for M1/M15 alerts. Arrissa API (self-hosted) for MT5 data, charts, news, calendar, and order execution. Perplexity AI for content generation and web search. SQLite for persistent memory, user profiles, and signal intelligence. Discord.js for delivery. All running on dedicated VPS infrastructure.
What is Signal Intelligence? ▶
Signal Intelligence is our self-learning market memory with verified outcome tracking. Every signal is captured, parsed, stored, and tracked to its result — WIN (TP hit), LOSS (SL hit), or EXPIRED (24h timeout). The system walks M15 candles every 15 minutes to verify outcomes. You can query win rates, net pips, hot instruments, grade breakdowns, session performance, and full signal history through the /signals command. When you ask the AI about any instrument, it automatically injects verified performance data into the conversation.
Can the AI analyze my screenshots? ▶
Yes. Send any trading-related image — chart screenshots, P&L results, trade logs, account balances — and the AI analyzes it visually using GPT-4o Vision. It reads your chart markup, identifies setups, spots mistakes, and gives you specific feedback based on what it sees. Works with @mentions and in auto-respond channels.
Does it support voice interaction? ▶
Yes. Send a voice message and the AI responds with voice. Your audio is transcribed by OpenAI Whisper, processed by GPT-4, and the response is synthesized as natural speech by ElevenLabs. Hands-free market analysis — ask questions about any instrument while you're away from your desk.

Every instrument tracked by Signal Intelligence with verified outcomes.
How does automated execution work? ▶
When the system generates a signal above the confidence threshold (65+), the Executor agent places the trade directly on your connected broker account. It calculates position size based on your risk parameters, sets stop loss and take profit, and manages the trade with progressive trailing stops. Supports MT5, MT4, Tradeovate, Trade Locker, and Liquid Broker. Premium gets Standard execution, VIP gets Priority, Elite gets Dedicated + Custom configurations.
Is it compatible with prop firm challenges? ▶
Yes. Risk Guardian is specifically designed for prop firm rules. It enforces daily loss limits, total drawdown caps, maximum trades per day, and 48-hour cooldowns after hitting thresholds. Works with FTMO, MyFundedFX, The5ers, TFT, and any prop firm that supports MT5/MT4.
What is the AI Mentor? ▶
