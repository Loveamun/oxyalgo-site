FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before joining.

How does copy trading work? ▶
When our AI agents execute a trade, it's automatically copied to your connected broker account in real time. You choose your lot sizes and risk level. Supported platforms: MetaTrader 5, MetaTrader 4, Tradeovate, Trade Locker, and Liquid Broker. Setup takes about 30 seconds.
What is the Discord bot (Amun)? ▶
Amun is a GPT-4 powered AI trading assistant that lives inside your Discord. It has access to 7 live market APIs, 68 entries of ICT/SMC knowledge, and remembers your conversations across sessions. You can ask it questions in natural language, request charts, check risk, and analyze setups — all with slash commands or by @mentioning it.
How do the 8 AI agents work? ▶
Each agent is a specialized automation powered by 34 workflows running on our infrastructure. The Scanner watches 27 instruments every 15 minutes. The Analyst scores setups using ICT/SMC. The Executor places trades. The Guardian manages risk. The Copier mirrors trades to your broker. The Intel agent delivers briefings. The Tracker logs performance. And Amun is your personal AI assistant for everything else.
What instruments do you cover? ▶
27+ instruments across 4 asset classes. Forex: 12 pairs including the GBP basket (EURGBP, GBPUSD, GBPJPY, GBPAUD, GBPCAD, GBPNZD, GBPCHF) plus EURUSD, USDJPY, DXY, AUDUSD, USDCAD. Crypto: BTC, ETH, XRP, SOL, BNB, DOGE, ADA, LINK, LTC, DOT. Indices: NAS100, US30, SPX500. Commodities: Gold, Crude Oil.
What's the founding member offer? ▶
The first 100 paid members lock in discounted pricing for life. Premium: $99/mo (normally $149), VIP: $249/mo (normally $349), Elite: $697/mo (normally $997). Your price never increases as long as you stay subscribed.
Can I use copy trading with my prop firm? ▶
Yes. Copy trading works with any prop firm that uses MT5, MT4, Tradeovate, or Trade Locker. This includes firms like FTMO, Topstep, Apex Trader Funding, The Funded Trader, and many more. You control the lot sizes and risk settings to stay within your firm's rules.
Can I cancel anytime? ▶
Yes. No contracts, no commitment. Cancel anytime from your dashboard. Access continues until the end of your billing period. No questions asked.
How is this different from other trading groups? ▶
Most trading groups are one person posting manual signals when they're online. We're an AI-powered trading operation — 8 specialized agents, 34 automated workflows running 24/5, copy trading across 5 platforms, a GPT-4 assistant, and full performance tracking. You're not joining a Discord group — you're hiring a trading team.
