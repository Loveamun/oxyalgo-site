n8n MCP Tools

Search 1100+ n8n nodes

Node schema + docs

n8n_autofix_workflow

Fire webhook, form, or chat triggers. Send test data and wait for response. Great for verifying workflows work before going live.

n8n_executions

Execution history

List, inspect, and debug workflow executions. Error mode gives optimized debugging with stack traces and upstream data.

n8n_deploy_template

Deploy from 2700+ templates

Pick a template from n8n.io and deploy directly to your instance. Auto-fixes common issues and upgrades node versions.

n8n_workflow_versions

Version control

Track workflow changes, rollback to previous versions, prune old versions. Never lose a working workflow configuration.

Kill Zone Schedule (EST)

London Kill Zone

1:30 - 4:00 AM

Manipulation phase. Watch for liquidity sweeps below Asian lows. Best for GBP basket trades. This is where the real money moves.

NY Kill Zone

7:30 - 10:30 AM

Distribution phase. Entries should align with London direction. Best for indices and Gold. Highest volume of the day.

Profit-Taking Window

10:00 - 11:00 AM

80% of the time, the opposite daily extreme posts here. Great for exits. Do NOT enter new trades in this window.

Risk Rules (Non-Negotiable)

2% Per Trade

Maximum risk per individual trade. Never exceed. Round lot size DOWN.

5% Daily Drawdown

If you hit 5% DD for the day, STOP trading. No exceptions. Come back tomorrow.

10% Total Drawdown

Max total drawdown on the account. If hit, stop and review everything.

News Block: 15 Minutes

No new trades within 15 minutes of high-impact news. Let the dust settle.

ICT/SMC Quick Reference

Power of 3 (PO3) Cycle

▶
1
Accumulation — Asian session. Price ranges. Smart money builds positions. Low volatility.
2
Manipulation — London open. Fake move to sweep liquidity (stops). Price spikes above/below the range to trap retail.
3
Distribution — Real move begins. Price expands in the intended direction. This is where you want to be positioned.

10-Factor Confluence Checklist

▶
1
HTF Bias Alignment — D1 + H4 agree with trade direction (MANDATORY)
2
Correct Zone — Buy in Discount, Sell in Premium (MANDATORY)
3
Valid Order Block — Last opposite-close candle before displacement near entry
4
OTE Level (62-79%) — Entry within Optimal Trade Entry zone. 70.5% is the sweet spot
5
IDM Swept — Liquidity gathered before the real move. Stops hunted first
6
Kill Zone Timing — Inside London or NY Kill Zone
7
FVG Present — Fair Value Gap near entry = institutional footprint
8
No News Blocking — No high-impact event within 15 min (MANDATORY)
9
PO3 Phase Correct — Entering at end of manipulation / start of distribution
10
Institutional Level — Near a round number (big fig or mid fig)

GBP Basket Logic

▶
↓
EUR/GBP FALLING — GBP is STRONG → BUY all GBP/XXX pairs
↑
EUR/GBP RISING — GBP is WEAK → SELL all GBP/XXX pairs
↔
EUR/GBP FLAT — GBP is NEUTRAL → NO BASKET TRADE
Risk Rule: Max 3 basket trades at once. Use 1.5% risk per trade (not 2%) to stay under 5% daily limit.
