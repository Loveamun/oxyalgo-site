ARCHITECTURE

Five Interconnected Layers

Each layer handles a distinct responsibility. Together, they form a fully autonomous trading business.

Layer 1: n8n Engine

42 workflows scan markets, generate signals, execute trades, post to Discord, track performance, and create social content. The backbone that runs everything on autopilot.

Layer 2: Discord Bot

Customer-facing AI assistant. Two-brain architecture (GPT-4 + Claude). 30 slash commands, @mention support, auto-respond, tier-gated access. 3 agent personas with distinct voices.

Layer 3: Intelligence

RAG knowledge base (68 ICT/SMC entries), user profiling, self-annotation, sales intelligence, psychology detection. The bot gets smarter with every interaction.

Layer 4: Signal Intelligence

Passive tracker captures every signal embed. SQLite stores 25+ fields per signal. Win rates, accuracy by session, grade breakdown, hot instruments. Self-learning market memory.

Layer 5: Content Bridge

Auto-generates viral short-form videos from real trading data. ~36 packages/week across 3 content types. Perplexity AI scripts, HeyGen avatar rendering, 9-platform distribution.
